# Project INGREAD
### Intelligent Recommendation Agent for Anti-fung Diet

<LoreContainer>
> The food supply chain was compromised. It had been that way for many years, and when we finally figured this out, the community and the Machinists rallied to prepare resources and machine AI agents to assist the identification and selection of foods to minimize exposure. It was codenamed Project INGREAD.

## What is INGREAD?
INGREAD can quickly **recognize and scan food ingredients** from a photo, **provide suggestions** and attempt to **answer food questions** related to NAC protocol. These types of questions are common when starting NAC protocol and INGREAD is designed to be a helpful assistant for this task.
