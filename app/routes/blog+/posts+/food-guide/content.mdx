## What is INGREAD?
INGREAD is the name of a tool that can quickly **recognize and scan food ingredients** from a photo, **provide suggestions** and attempt to **answer food questions** related to NAC protocol. These types of questions are common when starting NAC protocol and INGREAD is designed to be a helpful assistant for this task.
